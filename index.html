Gowanus Atlas
Mapping the contexts and stories around the Gowanus Canal.
Gowanus Atlas tells the story of the community around the Gowanus Canal, a neighborhood defined by its people, history, and diversity. It gives residents, business owners, organizations, activists, developers, and agencies access to quantitative and qualitative data about the area's rapidly changing conditions, its challenges, and the community's future. Gowanus Atlas is a resource for connection, participation, and activism.
Explore Gowanus Atlas

Gowanus Atlas is a project by Gowanus by Design. Interviews conducted by David Briggs and Elise McCurley. Atlas designed and built by Landscape Metrics, LLC
